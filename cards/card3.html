Access to food

Access to food is a basic human right and is essential for a healthy and fulfilling life. However, unfortunately, many people around the world do not have consistent and reliable access to adequate and nutritious food. Here are some ways to improve access to food:

● Support our food assistance program by helping us in providing meals to individuals and families who are struggling with food insecurity. You can support us by donating food or volunteering your time during collection and distribution

● Educate yourself and others: Understanding the root causes of food insecurity, such as poverty and systemic inequalities, can help us work towards long-term solutions. You can also educate others about the issue of food insecurity and encourage them to take action to support access to food for all.

● Before donating, make sure to check the expiration dates on any perishable items, and only donate food that is still safe to eat

● we will contact the community organizations such as churches, schools,beggars and homeless shelters that accept food donations and will also consider distributing it to the street animals.
